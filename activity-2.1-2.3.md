# Activity 2.1-2.3

2.1

Information includes both electronic and physical information. The process of information management encompasses collection, storage, gathering and organizing information, archiving and destruction. There are the benefits of IM: reducing costs and uncertainty or risks, adding value to existing products or services and creating new value through new information –based products or services. IT occupies an integral part of IM. However. Good information technology does not mean good information.
